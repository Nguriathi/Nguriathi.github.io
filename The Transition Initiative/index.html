Who are we?

The transition initiative team is a multifaceted team with various skills acquired at accredited learning institutions with the aim of invention and innovation and to give back to society by applying learnt skills in solving humanitarian problems in alignment with the Sustainable development goals (SDGs). Check out our gallery.

The Transition initiative is meant to create awareness about the Corona virus among local citizens of Baraton Centre. Each team member was voluntarily recruited, informed of our course and each gave their affirmative verbal informed consent. The founder formed a WhatsApp group, added members and the initiative reborn.
